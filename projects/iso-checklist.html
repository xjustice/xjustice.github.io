Compliance Meets Nostalgia

The ISO 17020 accreditation process is inherently rigorous and complex. Our goal with ISO Checklist XP was to simplify this journey by creating a highly structured checklist within a familiar, nostalgic user interface. This juxtaposition of "fun" visuals and "serious" compliance logic makes management less fatiguing.

Engineering User Focus

By using **IndexedDB**, the application ensures that all compliance data remains local and secure. We implemented a custom state management system that tracks ISO clause completion in real-time, providing immediate feedback on accreditation readiness.

Design Evolution

Even within a "Retro" theme, we applied modern **Apple-inspired alignment and spacing** to keep the interface highly legible. This hybrid approach allows for a playful exploration of history while delivering a professional tool capable of handling enterprise-level compliance checks.
